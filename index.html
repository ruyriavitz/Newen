About Newen

We are a network of innovation enthusiasts with deep experience in analyzing and implementing processes that help organizations become more open to technological solutions. Across all organizations, we have seen a recurring challenge: a significant gap between identifying a promising technology and successfully implementing it. Newen exists to close that gap.

Our Work

We help energy producers to:

  • Understand which technologies best fit their needs
  • Evaluate pilots
  • Coordinate implementation across stakeholders (Project Management)
  • Measure the impact of adopted technologies

We help technology providers to:

  • Find the right client in Latin America
  • Close contracts and supervise local operations
  • Provide top talent for execution
